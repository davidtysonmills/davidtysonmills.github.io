What is an Invoice?

An invoice is a document issued by a business or freelancer to request payment from a client for services or products provided. It includes details like services rendered, costs, and payment terms. Using a free invoice generator like Brevws or a free invoice template simplifies the process, ensuring your invoices look professional and are easy to manage.
