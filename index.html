What is an Invoice?

An invoice is a document sent by a business to a client requesting payment for goods or services. It details the products or services provided, quantities, prices, and payment terms. Using an invoice generator or free invoice template simplifies this process, helping businesses maintain professionalism and track payments efficiently.
