Frequently asked questions

Is an easy online invoice generator good for small businesses

Yes. It helps you produce clean, consistent invoices quickly. If you need more depth later, you can move to a full free invoicing software suite with client portals and automations.

What is the difference between an invoice generator and a professional invoice maker

An invoice generator focuses on speed and simplicity. A professional invoice maker adds features like templates, recurring billing, and branding controls.

Can I switch tools easily

Yes. Most tools export PDFs. Some also export CSV data so you can migrate items and client lists when you are ready.
